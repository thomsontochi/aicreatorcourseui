FREQUENTLY ASKED.
HONESTLY ANSWERED.

Anything missing? Email [email protected] — a real editor on our team answers within a day.

What is PromptEdit.com?

One place to access every major AI tool — image, video, audio, music, voice. Buy credits once, use them anywhere on the site. No subscription required.

Can I use the output commercially?

Yes — every generation is wrapped in a commercial license. Use it for social posts, client work, paid ads, the lot.

Is the quality different from the tool's own site?

No. We pipe through the same model APIs the source providers expose. Same output quality, one balance, no extra accounts.

What is the Content Creator Templates Library?

100,000+ professionally-designed editing assets — LUTs, SFX, text animations, overlays, animated backgrounds — included with the monthly plan. Drag-and-drop in any editor.

Are there limits on downloads?

No. Unlimited downloads. Unlimited usage. On the monthly plan you can pull every asset you need, on every project, forever.

Will the templates work in my editing platform?

Yes — Premiere Pro, DaVinci Resolve, Final Cut Pro, CapCut. Every pack is labelled with its compatible app upfront.

Can I cancel any time?

One click in your account. Within 30 days of signup, your unused credits get refunded too.

What happens to my downloaded templates if I cancel?

Anything you've already downloaded is yours to keep and keep using. Subscriptions gate access going forward — not what's already on your drive.
